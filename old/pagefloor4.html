What floor are we on?

A green floor, a grid of poker carpets that was orginally a scene from a collaboration with Lieselotte Vloeberghs. Together as the O Tapestry Tapestry Club they broadcasted a radio soap opera story that introduces listeners to this green landscape. The green square carpet often returns in 'DEVE FALLS', a online webseries. For the occasion of a screening exhibition in VDU Gallery in Kaunas (LT) the floor was re-enacted in the form of a backdrop. During the opening in Kaunas the O Tapestry Tapestry Club live streamed a live musical show.

ABOUT EARTH

An installation depicting a hypothesis that earth and it's twofold hemispheres have a cylindrical or octagonal shape. The work consists of two slide shows projected on two sponge objects. The slides are photographs of octagonal or cylindrical objects of the same sponge material. The work was included in 'The Soft Show' in Kolder, curated by Maria Correia. All the participants also contributed to a 'soft publication' by Henrique Martins. The same hypothesis was reproduced in two wind signs.

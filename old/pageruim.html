RUIM

A wooden installation, activated by a performer, following a story told by 2 voice-over tapes. The recordings are each a monologue of a floor and a ceiling contemplating on their existence. Playing both tapes simultaneously forms a dialogue that is then demonstrated by the changes made by the performer on the wooden maquette. The installation can be a tool/toy, used for any narrative on space. It comes with a manual .

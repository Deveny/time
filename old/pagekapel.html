'ALL WORK NO PLAY'


Together with Celine Aernoudt and Dido Woelders the exhibition 'ALL WORK NO PLAY' was held at the Saint-Quentinschapel in Ghent. The works suggested all kinds of game elements. The chapel had an outdoors playfield, which was repainted for a performance that incorporated some of the other art works, 'What Floor Are We On?'.

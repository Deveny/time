SUN PIECE

An wax sun, carefully melted from different tints and kinds of yellow wax. After finding the right combination of colour and consistency the entire sun was melted for a short experimental video. The leftovers were melted again into the same sun-shape for an installation for the exhibition, 'ALL WORK NO PLAY' in the Saint-Quentins chapel in Ghent, where it was being melted every moment

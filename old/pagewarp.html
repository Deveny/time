Together with Yonas Seyoum and Roel Kerhofs the house and the exhibition space of WARP in Sint-Niklaas was filled with several works and scenes. Yonas Seyoum studied Mixed Media too, Roel Kerkhofs is a teacher at this department.

A travelling checkered floor was installed on a stage along which microphones with their standards were installed. When standing closely to these mics, you could hear songs made for the exhibition and for the monotonous chord coming from the top floor of the building filled most of the spaces. On the top floor a blue carpet had been layed to connect with Yonas' projections in the other rooms of the building. The blue room was arranged with both works of Roel and Deveny.

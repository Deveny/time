What floor are we on?

Out of the several game-inspired sculptures/installations in the past a checkered floor maquette existed. What was at first a maquette based on a chessboard or draugtboard and afterwards became a portable floor made out of rubber black squares that can convert any floor to a checkered floor. It can be layered on top of any other existing floor. The floor was once used in WARP too to transform a stage. The traveling checkered floor finds it foundation in Jorge Luis Borges' 'Garden of Forked Paths'.
